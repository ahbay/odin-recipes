Turkey Salad

Perfect solution for Thanksgiving leftovers

This turkey salad recipe is a great spread for sandwiches or crackers. If you don't know what to do with leftover turkey, this is perfect!

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Vegetables: celery, green onions, red bell pepper
  • Mayo and mustard
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pulse the turkey and vegetables in a food processor until finely chopped.
  2. Transfer to a bowlm, then mix in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Cover and chill in the fridge for eight hours to allow the lavors to meld.
