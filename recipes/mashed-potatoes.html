Mashed Potatoes

If you don't eat mashed potatoes on Thanksgiving, you are not real.

This awesome sauce mashed potatoes recipe will fulfill all your potato desires!

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and halved
  • 1/2 mascarpone cheese at room temperature
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • some cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup butter cut into chunks

Directions

  1. Put the potato halves into a pot of salted water until it boils, and reduce heat to medium-low. Cook until thats very tender. A knife should pierce the middle of a potato half with ease. Transfer the potatoes to a colander and drain thoroughly.
  2. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees C (425 degrees F).
  3. Whisk mascarpone chees in a mixing bowl until smooth.
  4. Stir egg yolk and milk into the cheese, whisking until smooth.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  6. Transfer potatoes to a large mixing bowl, add butter chunks to the bowl, and season with salt and black pepper.
  7. Mash butter into potatoes until fluffy, a few little lumps are okay. Whisk mascarpone mixture into the mashed potatoes.
  8. Spread the mashed potatoes into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Smooth the top and if you want to be extra use the tip of a spatula to press the potatoes and l ift up to craete litle peaks and valleys.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are heated through and the top is golden brown.
  10. You are done enjoy your mashed potatoes.
